# Companies Hiring for Robotics

### **Companies Hiring for Robotics: India and Global Opportunities** <a href="#undefined" id="undefined"></a>

The robotics industry is experiencing rapid growth, creating a diverse range of job opportunities for skilled professionals across India and globally. Companies are actively seeking talent in areas from core engineering and AI development to specialized fields like SLAM, Haptics, and Human-Robot Interaction. This section provides an overview of various job roles in robotics and examples of companies that typically hire for these positions in both Indian and international markets.

_(Note: Company hiring needs are dynamic. This list provides examples of companies known to operate in these domains and is not exhaustive. Always check company career pages and job portals for current openings.)_
